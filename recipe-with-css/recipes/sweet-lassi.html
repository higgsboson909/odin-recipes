Sweet Lassi (Yogurt Drink)

Description

Sweet lassi is the perfect summer drink that comes together in less than five minutes. Plain lassi is easy to forget but every time I have it, I am reminded of how satisfying it is during a hot day. While most people outside South Asia only know mango lassi, I grew up with plain yogurt lassi. It’s lighter, serving as an actual refreshment rather than a dessert. The recipe I share here is my mother’s who is Punjabi with roots in Ludhiana in eastern Punjab

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cup yogurt
  • 3 cups water
  • 6 ice cubes
  • 4 tablespoons sugar

Steps

  1. Add ice cubs and yogurt to a blender, and blend on low speed for 20 – 30 seconds until frothy.
  2. Add water and sugar to the yogurt and ice cube mixture and blend on low speed until well-mixed.
